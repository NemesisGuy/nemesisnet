How We Scope Projects

Every system is classified based on complexity, business risk, data sensitivity, and workflow requirements. We build right-sized solutions — no overengineering, no underbuilding.

Tier 1

Lightweight Systems

Fast build for MVPs, internal tools, automation, and simple workflows.

StackVue/React + PocketBase + SQLite
ComplexityLow — single dev maintainable
Delivery1–5 days
ExamplesForms, Dashboards, CRUD, Simple Auth
Tier 2

Business Systems

Standard engineering for multi-user tools, permissions, and API integrations.

StackSpring Boot + MySQL/PostgreSQL + Vue
ComplexityMedium — structured architecture
Delivery1–3 weeks
ExamplesClient Portals, CMS, Business Tools
Tier 3

Core / Enterprise

High complexity for financial systems, CRMs, and scalable enterprise platforms.

StackSpring Boot DDD + Redis + Message Queues
ComplexityHigh — full CI/CD, microservices
Delivery1–3+ months
ExamplesCRMs, Billing, Financial Systems

How Projects Work

A structured process keeps delivery predictable, technical risk visible, and decisions aligned before engineering time is burned.

Without proper scoping, projects usually drift into scope creep, deadline slips, and expensive rework.

Step 1

Get In Touch

Reach out via the contact form, email us directly, or submit your full project brief at scope.nemesisnet.co.za. We respond to every enquiry within 72 hours.

Step 2

Requirements & Review

We review your brief and assess scope, architecture fit, and delivery timeline. If needed, we schedule a short discovery call to clarify requirements before any engineering time is spent.

Step 3

Proposal & Agreement

We send a tailored proposal with a fixed price, milestones, and delivery timeline. Once agreed, we get started immediately.

Step 4

Build & Deliver

Development starts with testing and CI/CD from day one. Regular checkpoints keep delivery aligned with scope until production handoff.
