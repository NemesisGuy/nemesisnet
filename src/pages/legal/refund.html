Refund And Cancellation Policy

Updated: 14 April 2026

1. Overview

NemesisNet provides custom software development, consulting, and SaaS-based services. Due to the nature of digital and custom-built solutions, refunds are handled case-by-case under the terms below.

2. Eligibility For Refunds

  • Project has not yet started.
  • Service cannot be delivered due to technical or operational constraints.
  • Mutual written agreement between NemesisNet and the client.
  • For subscriptions, requests must be submitted within 14 days of initial purchase and before substantial use.

3. Non-Refundable Services

  • Completed or partially completed custom development work.
  • Delivered digital products, source code, or deployments.
  • Consultation or advisory sessions already conducted.
  • Any service where significant time or resources have been allocated.

4. Partial Refunds

If a project is cancelled mid-way, partial refunds may be issued based on the percentage of work completed and at NemesisNet's discretion.

5. Refund Process

  • Contact: admin@nemesisnet.co.za
  • Include project or transaction reference.
  • Include reason for request.

Approved refunds are processed via the original payment method. Processing fees may not be refundable where retained by payment providers.

6. Processing Time And Cancellations

  • Refunds may take 3-10 business days after approval.
  • Before project start: full refund.
  • After project start: billed proportionally to completed work.

Subscription services can be cancelled at any time, but cancellations do not guarantee retroactive refunds.

Disputes: clients are encouraged to contact us first to resolve concerns before initiating chargebacks or formal disputes.
