Bored Room Cafe

A local homestyle takeaway and food-service spot in Ferness Estate.

The Challenge

Small local businesses often struggle with digital visibility and streamlined ordering. The goal was to build a professional online presence that showcases the local flavor while providing a simple, effective way for families to order food.

The Solution

A clean, high-performance website focused on accessibility and visual appeal. By emphasizing local branding and a user-centric ordering flow, I helped the cafe transition from purely physical presence to a digital-first local favorite.

Technical Implementation

Built using a modern static architecture to ensure instant load times on mobile devices. The site focuses on high-impact imagery and a streamlined UX to drive conversions from social media traffic to direct orders.
