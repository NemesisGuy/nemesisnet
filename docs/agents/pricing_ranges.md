## Guardrails (To Protect Margin)

- All values are starting-from pricing.
- Discovery + scope confirmation is mandatory before final quote.
- Change requests outside scope are billed hourly.
- Suggested hourly bands: general senior engineering R850-R1,300/hour.
- Suggested hourly bands: advanced AI/specialized work R1,200-R1,500/hour.

## Package Notes

### Starter / Rapid Delivery

- Typical scope: lean portal/MVP, infra baseline, deployment hardening.
- Internal effort guardrail: roughly 12-18 focused dev hours for the base package.
- Typical timeline: 2-4 weeks.

### Pro Full-Stack Platform

- Typical scope: multi-user platform, auth, API + frontend integration, testing.
- Delivery model: milestone-based with scoped acceptance criteria.
- Typical timeline: 6-14 weeks depending on scope.

### AI-Powered and Advanced Tooling

- Typical scope: model integration, self-hosting, optimization, observability, security review.
- Assumes elevated complexity and longer stabilization cycle.
- Typical timeline: 8-16 weeks, scoped after technical discovery.

### Maintenance and Retainers

- Light Hosting and Updates is the low-friction option for smaller budgets and stable sites.
- Managed Engineering Retainer is for active products needing ongoing feature and reliability work.
- Additional support hours outside retainer scope are charged separately.

### Custom MCP and Agent Integrations

- Position this as platform-agnostic integration engineering, not a single-product plugin service.
- Mention CMS, CRM, and internal tooling capabilities in public copy to widen perceived value.
- Typical timeline: 3-6 weeks per integration.

## Recommended Website Copy Rules

- Use language like "Starting from" (never "fixed").
- Include a short line: "Final quote follows discovery and scope.".
- Keep public pricing to package-level signals; keep detailed effort math internal.
